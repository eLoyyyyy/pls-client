PCN in Focus

Who We Are

We are the pioneers in the promotions marketing industry, having been formed in 1988 as an advertising firm under the name, Promotions & Communications Network.

Due to a growing industry demand for more focused services on below-the-line activities, with ownership changes within the organization came a change in company direction and name. Thus, we became PCN Promopro, Inc.

Since then, we have grown in size, business volume and clientele, with lasting partnerships with Unilever Philippines, Inc., PMFTC Inc., Sun Cellular, and the like.
